> Development of a modern and effective showcase website for a cleaning company in the Hurepoix region

**Description:**

I had the pleasure of designing and developing the website for Nettoyage Du Hurepoix, a cleaning services company located in the Greater Paris Metropolitan Area. The project aimed to create a professional and effective online presence to attract new clients.

**Technologies and Tools Used:**

- WordPress as CMS
- Divi Theme for advanced customization
- SEO optimization for local referencing
- Integrated contact and quote forms
- Responsive and modern design

**Key Features:**

- Intuitive and professional user interface
- Clear presentation of cleaning services
- Online quote system
- Local search optimization
- Responsive design for all devices
- Integration of contact details and service areas
- Blog section for tips and advice

**Results:**

The site provides a professional showcase that highlights the company's services while facilitating contact with potential clients. The focus was on:

- Smooth and intuitive navigation
- Clear presentation of services
- Strategically placed calls to action
- Local SEO optimization
