**Description:**

As a front-end developer at Artefact 3000, I participated in the design and development of an interactive application for Argedis, a TotalEnergies subsidiary. This application was deployed on tablets available in service stations to promote local producers.

Users can view an interactive map showing the station where they are, surrounded by partner local producers, with their distance from the station.

**Technologies and Tools Used:**

- **Next.js (v 13: App Router)** for asynchronous management in server components and optimal performance.
- **Contentful (Headless CMS)** for content management: photos, texts, and producer coordinates.
- **GraphQL** for structured and efficient API calls.
- **Tailwind CSS** for modern and responsive design.
- **PWA and APK**: initially a PWA, converted to APK for tablet OS compatibility.

**Key Features:**

- **Interactive Map**: Precise positioning of local producers on a regional map with `x` and `y` coordinates, stored in Contentful.
- **Offline Compatibility**: Implementation of a static and offline-accessible application.
- **Optimized Deployment**: Conversion to APK for direct installation on stations' Android tablets.
- **Smooth User Experience**: Intuitive navigation between supplier cards and regional maps.

**Challenges and Solutions:**

- **Position Management**: Pixel-perfect positioning of producers on the regional map
- **Maintenance and Updates**: Frequent addition and modification of producers in Contentful was repetitive and tedious, representing an area for improvement.
- **Android Compatibility**: Transition from PWA to APK to bypass tablet installation limitations.

**Results:**

The application helped strengthen the visibility of local producers and improve customer experience in Total stations. This project, deployed nationally, reached hundreds of producers and highlighted regional specialties in a clear and engaging interface.
