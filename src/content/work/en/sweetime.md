**Context:**

For Extime, Paris Airports' luxury brand, I developed an innovative digital contest allowing travelers to win discounts and gifts in airport shops.

**Technical Solution:**

A fully responsive mobile web application that transforms the airport experience into a fun and interactive adventure.

**Key Technologies:**

- **Next.js 13** with App Router
- **Tailwind CSS** for design
- **MongoDB/Mongoose** for data management
- **Docker** for deployment and testing
- **i18n** for bilingual support (FR/EN)

**Innovative Features:**

- Winning ticket generation system with probabilistic algorithms
- Virtual scratching mechanism with animations
- Unique user validation (email, cookies)
- Complete multilingualism
- Integration with Extime shops' discount systems

**Technical Challenges:**

- Design of a precise randomization algorithm
- Management of variable winning probabilities
- Security against multiple participations
- Performance optimization on mobile devices

**Impact:**

- Increased customer engagement in airport shops
- Innovative user experience
- Enhancement of Extime's offering

**Results:**

- Deployment across all Roissy terminals
- Several hundred thousand participants
- Engagement rate exceeding expectations
